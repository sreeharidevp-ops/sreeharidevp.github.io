Maintenance & Service

Generator maintenance frequency depends on usage:
  • Standby generators (rarely used): Service every 6 months or 100 running hours
  • Prime power generators (frequent use): Service every 250-500 running hours or quarterly
  • Continuous operation: Monthly service and load testing
We also recommend weekly visual inspections and monthly test runs for all standby units to ensure they're ready when needed.

Our comprehensive preventive maintenance includes:
  • Engine oil and filter replacement
  • Fuel filter replacement
  • Air filter inspection and replacement
  • Coolant system check and top-up
  • Battery testing and terminal cleaning
  • Belt inspection and adjustment
  • Electrical connections inspection
  • Load testing and performance verification
  • Complete service report with recommendations

Yes, we service and maintain generators from all major manufacturers, regardless of where they were purchased. Our technicians are trained on multiple brands and have access to genuine parts for most common generator models in the UAE market.

Yes, we offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC) with scheduled preventive maintenance, priority emergency support, discounted parts, and performance reporting. AMC packages ensure your generators are always ready and help extend equipment life while reducing unexpected repair costs.

Technical Support & Repairs

If your generator won't start, check these basics first:
  • Ensure there's adequate fuel in the tank
  • Check battery connections and voltage
  • Verify the emergency stop button isn't engaged
  • Check for any fault codes on the control panel
If the issue persists, contact our 24/7 emergency support immediately. Do not attempt complex repairs yourself as this may void warranties.

Yes, we use genuine OEM parts from authorized distributors whenever possible. For critical components like fuel injectors, turbochargers, and control modules, we exclusively use manufacturer-approved parts to ensure reliability, performance, and warranty compliance.

Repair time depends on the issue complexity and parts availability:
  • Minor repairs: 2-4 hours (filter changes, battery replacement, minor adjustments)
  • Moderate repairs: 1-2 days (electrical troubleshooting, fuel system repairs)
  • Major overhauls: 3-7 days (engine rebuilds, major component replacement)
We always provide estimated repair time and keep you updated on progress.
